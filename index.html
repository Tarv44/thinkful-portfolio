About Jonah

Although I love touring and playing on stages, I’ve always strived to find productive ways to spend the long hours on the road and the time in between tours. I’ve been involved in many creative projects, albeit mostly music-centered. Through working with other artists, music labels, and creative startups, I started noticing how many creative people need a lot of help with the more technical side of promoting their work online.

Due to a desire to help fulfill this need (and, more recently, a pandemic that put much of my life as a performer on pause), I’ve committed much of my time and energy to master the skills required to be a reliable developer. I’m currently enrolled in Thinkful’s Engineering program (expecting to graduate end of March) and. I’ve completed 70+ hours of Web Development lessons through Team Treehouse and Free Code Camp.

I’m now seeking new opportunities to work on projects of all kinds. Whether it’s working alongside a talented team of programmers, learning quickly and producing results, or helping those with artistic visions realize their goals, I’m up for the task!

Anyhoo, here are some of the things I can help you with:

  • HTML/CSS
  • Javascript
  • Node.js
  • React
  • Data Structures and Algorithms
